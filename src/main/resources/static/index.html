How It Works

Principal: The initial amount of money invested.

Rate: The annual rate of return on investment.

Time: The number of years the money will be invested.

Contribution per month: The additional amount added every month.

Future Value: The estimated total investment value before taxes and inflation.

Graph Interpretation:

  • Compound Interest Growth: Shows how the investment grows over time.
  • Total Amount Invested: Represents principal + contributions.
  • Inflation-Adjusted Value: Adjusts for an average 3.1% yearly inflation.
